About Socialnet

Welcome to Socialnet, the next-generation social network built with Django and styled with TailwindCSS. Designed with pure performance and User Experience in mind for 2026.

Our Mission

Our mission is to connect people quickly, efficiently, and with a visually delightful experience. We break away from heavy and slow interfaces by embracing a clean architecture based on the Bento Grid concept and Liquid Glass aesthetics for a pleasant, organized, and relaxing browsing experience.

Our Technical Pillars

  • Superior Performance: Mandatory Core Web Vitals compliance with LCP < 2.0s and zero interaction delay (INP).
  • Native OLED Dark Mode: Factory-level light reduction and energy savings through adaptive dark mode focused on the #121212 color.
  • Organic Google Monetization: Ad blocks with strictly reserved sizes (`min-height`) to deliver zero layout shifts (CLS) in a natural and legitimate way.

Socialnet’s infrastructure values Silo Structure and semantic HTML in Django templates to achieve top search engine rankings (SEO).
