Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 23, 2026

Your privacy is critically important to us at Socialnet. This policy was written with transparency in mind and is designed to comply with advertising partners and agencies, including Google AdSense.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide better services to all of our users. This includes:

  • Information you provide directly (e.g., your name and email for account registration).
  • Information collected from your use of our services (e.g., log data, device location, and interaction history with visual content/posts).
  • Cookies and anonymous primary identifiers for analytics and navigation tracking.

2. Use of Personal Data

We use modern Django infrastructure combined with secure relational databases to store essential information, focusing on:

  • Maintaining your access through persistent web sessions or Single Sign-On (SSO).
  • Preventing fraud and maintaining the integrity of the social platform.
  • Delivering organic and optimized Native Ads through the AdSense ecosystem without penalizing your Core Web Vitals.

3. Your Rights

You have full control over your information. Any logged-in user can download, process, modify, or complete a full deletion (`Hard Delete`) of their account and all hosted media with the click of a button in the Settings panel.

4. Third Parties and Advertising

To keep our servers running fast and reliably in a complex Bento Grid and optimized Liquid Glass environment, we display ads in partnership with Google. Third-party providers use cookies to serve ads based on your previous visits. You have the right to opt out of these personalized advertising settings in your browser’s global advertising preferences.
